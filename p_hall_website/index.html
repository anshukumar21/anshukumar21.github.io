Frequently Asked Questions

There are no such restrictions. You are free to enter or exit the hall premises anytime. However, remember to take your ID Card with you at all times.

There is at least one water cooler and one washing machine in every block. Potable water is also available next to the hall mess.

Rooms are cleaned twice a week. However, if your room gets dirty, you may call a sweeper anytime and get it cleaned.

There are three Wardens of the Hall. You may contact them via email or meet them in the Warden Office (if available). Their contact details are as follows:

You can contact the Maintenance Secretary, Shashwat Dwivedi (7007508261) or lodge a complaint in the Hall Office. You may also complain online at pingala.iitk.ac.in regarding any maintenance issue.
